What is MySatelit?

MySatelit is a mobile application designed to help users efficiently book appointments before visiting a satellite office. Users will receive an email notification after booking their appointment, making it easier to secure a time slot in advance. Additionally, users can leave comments to help improve office services.

Key Features:

  • ✅ Easy appointment booking system – Schedule appointments in just a few taps.
  • ✅ Real-time satellite tracking – View your booked appointments on a calendar.
  • ✅ Email notifications – Receive confirmation emails upon booking.
